To use the app, you can start by printing a new item by clicking the "Print" button.

ITEMS THAT THE PRINTER CAN PRINT:

  • Spinaches
  • Tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Onions

HOW LONG IT WILL TAKE FOR NUMBER OF ITEMS TO BE PRINTED:

  • 25 (3 minutes to print)
  • 45 (8 minutes to print)
  • 65 (17 minutes to print)
  • 85 (25 minutes to print)
